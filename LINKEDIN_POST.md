## Posting Tips:

### Best Time to Post:
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 9-10 AM or 12-1 PM (your timezone)
- **Avoid**: Weekends and Monday mornings

### Video Specs for LinkedIn:
- **Format:** MP4
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080) recommended
- **Length:** 30-90 seconds (sweet spot: 45 seconds)
- **Size:** Under 200 MB
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 for desktop, 1:1 for mobile
- **Captions:** Always add (85% watch without sound)

### Engagement Boosters:
1. **First line is critical** - Make it grab attention
2. **Ask a question** at the end to encourage comments
3. **Use 3-5 relevant hashtags** (more looks spammy)
4. **Tag relevant people/companies** if applicable
5. **Respond to ALL comments** within first hour
6. **Share to your story** for extra reach
