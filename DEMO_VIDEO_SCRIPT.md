## Recording Tips:
1. **Use Chrome browser** in incognito mode for clean recording
2. **Record at 1080p** (1920x1080) for clarity
3. **Use smooth cursor movements** - no quick jerks
4. **Add subtle background music** - something upbeat but professional
5. **Keep it under 60 seconds** for maximum LinkedIn engagement
6. **Add captions/subtitles** - 85% of LinkedIn videos watched without sound

## Tools You Can Use:
- **Screen Recording:** OBS Studio (free), Loom, or ScreenFlow
- **Video Editing:** DaVinci Resolve (free), iMovie, or Camtasia
- **Text Overlays:** CapCut (free), Canva Video, or Adobe Premiere
- **Music:** YouTube Audio Library or Epidemic Sound

## Quick Alternative (No Face/Voice):
Record screen with these steps and add:
- **Text overlays** at each scene transition
- **Subtle zoom effects** to highlight important features
- **Background music** (royalty-free)
